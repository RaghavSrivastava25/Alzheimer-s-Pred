THE STRESS-PASSERS

ABOUT

THE STRESS-PASSERS is an a initiative cum project started by us few students of IILM UNIVERSITY GREATER NOIDA. We are planning to be build a open source software and website for people suffering from mental health related issues. The begging of the initiative will involve day to day blogs of how to handle stress of all kinds. Tips and advices to increase your willpower , mental resistance , sharpen your memory , also case studies and personal life stories of people who experienced a subtle but personally drastic changes in their mental health and were able to come out of it. Further the initiative will come up with a trained model which can read MRI scans of patients with chronic mental or brain related issues, there by saving costs and time to get an endless chain chain of appointments to the hospitals. In future the page will be open to everyone , people can share their stories, advices , modify the model and interact with each other. Resulting in a self sustained helpoing environment.

WHAT WE AIM TO PROVIDE PEOPLE


*idea of "OPEN SOURCE MRI SCANNER":

People suffer a lot for getting their mri scannes done and also the reports are not more accurate if not done properly or due to lack of more precise machinery. India has a few number of trusted labs who are performing such high accuracy demanding scans,this has resulted in monopoly of such few companies over this and has converted this serious health issue more into their marketing field and making huge profits. We "THE STRESS-PASSERS" have come up with this public welfare approach to develope an open source MRI SCANNER, which will be available to everyone. Also the model will be self evolving over the period to predict in which portion of the brain the patient is suffering and accordingly suggest cure.This is help people of all strata to have health benefits.
